About KIMSU Specialist Centre

Specialist urology services in Meru — caring, precise and modern.

Who we are

KIMSU Specialist Centre is a specialist hospital in Meru focusing on urology services. We operate near Kenya Methodist University's main campus and are growing to include more specialties.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Deliver safe, evidence-based specialist care with dignity and compassion.
Vision: Be the leading specialist centre in Meru for urology and related services.

Our journey

  • 2024 – Founded and launched specialist urology services.
  • 2025 – Expanded outpatient and diagnostics services.
