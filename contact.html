How to make an appointment

  1. Call reception or use the online booking form.
  2. Tell us the reason for the visit so we can allocate the right time and clinician.
  3. Bring any previous test results or medication list.

Referrals

We accept referrals from local clinics and hospitals. Email scanned referral letters to kimsuspecialisthosp@gmail.com and indicate urgent requests in the subject line.

Parking & access

Free short-term parking is available on-site. The clinic is wheelchair-accessible with ground-floor entry.
